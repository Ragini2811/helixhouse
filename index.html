Biodiversity Indices Explanation

The Circle Chart

The purple portion represents the variety of species present.

The Line Graph

The x-axis : different samples or sites.

The y-axis : the diversity score at each site.

A higher point means more diversity at that site, while a lower point means less diversity.

Shannon Index (H)

This shows how mixed the species are. A high score means there are many different species, and they exist in good balance.

Simpson Index (Œª)

This shows the chance of picking two different species randomly. If the score is close to 1, it means there is a lot of variety.

Species Richness (S)

This is simply the count of different species present.
